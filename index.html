About Jonathan Victor

Jonathan M. Victor, Esq. grew up in Mahopac and has been practicing law for more than eight years. Admitted to practice in New York, Connecticut and Florida state courts as well as several federal courts, he combines local knowledge with broad legal experience. Our practice is built around a client‑centred philosophy – after listening to your goals and concerns, we offer our advice and empower you to make the final decision.
